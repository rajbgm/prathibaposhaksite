Countless children possessing the talent, tenacity, and passion to pursue studies in Mathematics, Science, Engineering, or Medicine are unjustly deprived of quality education, guidance, and financial support. Pratibha Poshak aims to bridge this gap by identifying exceptionally gifted students, even from remote rural areas and urban slums, and digitally connecting them with mentors and teachers, enabling access to top-notch online education.
