Founding Members

Dr. Shashikant Y. Kulgod
MS., FRCS(Ed), FRCS(Glasg)

Dr. Shashikant is a surgical gastroenterologist and a Director of the BHS Lakeview Hospitals, Belagavi. He has immense interest in strengthening government schools and promoting health education among children. He founded the Rajalakshmi Children Foundation, and has been spearheading its various initiatives.

Dr. Ravindra N. Guravannavar
M.Tech., Ph.D. (IIT Bombay)

Dr. Ravindra, a former faculty member of IIT Hyderabad, is a computer scientist specializing in the areas of large scale data management and distributed systems, and works as an independent consultant in the software industry. He enjoys spreading the joy of Mathematics and Computing among school children. He initiated the Sharada Pratibha Poshan Upakram and the Pratibha Poshak project.

Dr. Vijayalakshmi S. Kulgod
MD. (Paed), MRCPCH (London)

Dr. Vijayalakshmi is a pediatrician at the BHS Lake View Hospitals, Belagavi. She co-founded the Rajalakshmi Children Foundation and leads the child health care services initiatives of RCF.

Board of Advisors

Dr. Rina Panigrahy
PhD (Stanford University)

Dr. Rina Panigrahy is a Research Scientist at Google. He works on theoretical and applied algorithms for machine learning, prediction and large graph analysis. He obtained B.Tech. (CS) from IIT Bombay, M.S. from Massachusetts Institute of Technology, and Ph.D. from Stanford University. He won a Gold Medal at the 31st International Mathematics Olympiad, 1990, Beijing, and the top rank (AIR 1) in JEE 1990. Dr. Panigrahy is interested in promoting Mathematics education, and nurturing talent. He has been closely associated with Pratibha Poshak since its inception.

More about Dr. Rina Panigrahy: http://theory.stanford.edu/~rinap/

Dr. Aruna Pawashe
PhD

Dr. Aruna Pawashe is a faculty member in the Molecular Biophysics and Biochemistry department at the Yale School of Medicine. A very caring, compassionate and attentive teacher with three decades of teaching experience is now dedicating a significant part of her time to promote STEM education and building modern infrastructure in Indian public schools. She initiated the buddy program: to connect children face to face in real time from different parts of the world, to help them understand each other’s living conditions, economic backgrounds, culture, heritage, dress, language and ethnicity, so as to help improve the sense of “Belonging”. Dr. Aruna is a patron of Pratibha Poshak and has been mentoring several students.

More about Dr. Aruna Pawashe: : https://medicine.yale.edu/profile/aruna-pawashe/

Dr. Sarasija Suresh
M.Pharm, Ph.D.

Dr. Sarasija is a Project Director at the Institute for Drug Delivery and Biomedical Research (IDBR), Bangalore and also a co-founder of RGV Research & Innovations. Dr. Sarasija obtained M. Pharm from Government College of Pharmacy, Bangalore and PhD from College of Pharmaceutical Sciences, Manipal. She has 28 years of teaching and research experience, and has taught in the National Institute of Pharmaceutical Education and Research (NIPER), Mohali and M.S. Ramaiah University of Applied Sciences. A recipient of several national and international fellowships and awards, Dr. Sarasija has more than a hundred research publications, several patents and has authored two books. Dr. Sarasija loves teaching Chemistry and has been devoting her time to teach the subject to children of Pratibha Poshak.
